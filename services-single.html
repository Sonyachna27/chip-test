What is included in the Local moving

01

Local Residential Moving Solutions

Nobody knows home moving like College HUNKS. With over 15 years of experience providing professional, comprehensive residential moving services to 200+ cities nationwide, our HUNKS are well-equipped to handle any local home move, big or small. We carefully package, wrap, and place every item in our trucks before moving them to your new location where we assist with unloading, unpacking, and arranging heavy furniture to ensure you’re successfully moved.

02

Local Commercial Movers

Moving your office or business can be stressful, that’s why we offer local business relocation services at the same standards we hold our home or apartment moving to to ensure every aspect of your relocation experience is seamless and easy. Our commercial clients can experience 5-star service and professionally trained movers to help with moving desks, office chairs, conference tables, computers, and more across town or across the state.

03

Local Apartment Moving

Moving into a new rental across town or even across the hall? Get in touch with a College HUNK near you to get a free, no obligation quote on our professional apartment moving services. Whether you need comprehensive services for your move or hourly moving help, like just packing, loading and unloading, or moving boxes, our teams are ready to help.

04

Local Moving Solution For Seniors

Moving an elderly loved one into a smaller home, retirement community, assisted living facility, or even into your own home can be stressful. Let College HUNKS make the transition easier for you and your family member by offering full sorting, packing, loading, transporting, unloading, unpacking, and arranging services to meet your needs. Our teams of professional movers are trained in all aspects of senior moving to make everyone feel comfortable in their new home.

Need to donate or recycle unwanted items? Our eco-friendly junk removal services and partnerships with local organizations like Goodwill and Habitat for Humanity ReStore® will help you responsibly donate any unwanted items.
